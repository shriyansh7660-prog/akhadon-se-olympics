About Indian Wrestling Heritage

Welcome to a digital celebration of India's rich wrestling tradition. This curated platform showcases the legendary wrestlers, traditional akhadas, and the evolution of Indian wrestling from ancient times to modern Olympic glory.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to preserving and sharing the stories of India's wrestling heroes. From the traditional mud arenas of village akhadas to the international wrestling mats, our mission is to honor the legacy of wrestlers who have brought pride to the nation.

The Art of Indian Wrestling

Indian wrestling, known as "Pehlwani" or "Kushti," is more than just a sport—it's a way of life. Rooted in ancient traditions, it emphasizes discipline, respect, and the holistic development of mind, body, and spirit. The traditional akhada serves not just as a training ground, but as a cultural institution that has preserved these values for centuries.

Join Our Community

Whether you're a wrestling enthusiast, a student of Indian culture, or someone interested in sports history, we invite you to explore these stories and contribute to our growing archive. Share your own stories, photos, or knowledge to help us build a comprehensive resource for future generations.

Contact & Contributions

We welcome contributions of historical photographs, wrestler biographies, and stories from the wrestling community. Please ensure all submissions are high-resolution and properly credited. Together, we can preserve this incredible heritage for generations to come.
